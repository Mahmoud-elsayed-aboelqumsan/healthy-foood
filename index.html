What about healthy food?

A person always needs healthy food, which is a balanced food that contains many different nutrients and vitamins that the body needs. It also protects the body from exposure to many diseases, including serious diseases such as cancer and chronic diseases as well

Public health experts believe that health and beauty are based mainly on the rules of proper nutrition, as the work and harmony of the body's organs depend on the balance of the essential elements contained in it through the food intake. It is well known that food consists mainly of the basic food groups

Although most foods contain a variety of nutrients, at the same time we do not find a single food that can contain all the nutrients, so nutrition and public health experts advise the need to eat different types of food without being limited to one type only
